Call for Papers

We invite submissions on socially-intelligent AI spanning reasoning, multimodality, interaction, and embodiment. Topics include (but are not limited to): social perception, affect & behavior modeling, human-AI collaboration, theory-of-mind in AI, multi-agent interaction, safety & ethics in social AI, benchmarks, and applications.
